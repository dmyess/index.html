About Leonardo DiCaprio

Leonardo Wilhelm DiCaprio (born November 11, 1974) is an American actor and film producer, known for his versatility in biographical and period films. He has earned numerous awards, including an Academy Award and multiple Golden Globes.

Career

  1. Early TV and commercial roles in the late 1980s and early 1990s.
  2. Breakthrough: What's Eating Gilbert Grape (1993).
  3. Worldwide fame after Titanic (1997).
  4. Later awards and collaborations: The Aviator, The Wolf of Wall Street, The Revenant.

Filmography / Key Achievements

  • First Oscar nomination: What's Eating Gilbert Grape (1993).
  • Titanic (1997) — global fame.
  • Frequent collaboration with Martin Scorsese.
  • Oscar for Best Actor — The Revenant (2015).
